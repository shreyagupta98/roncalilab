Roncali Lab

The Roncali lab is part of the Department of Biomedical Engineering and the Department of Radiology.

Applying a biomedical engineering perspective to solve clinical problems, we conduct highly translational and interdisciplinary research in close collaboration with clinicians at UC Davis Health and other institutions.

We focus on modeling and simulation for medical imaging and therapy physics, and translating the benefits of technological advances to improving the care of our patients. Please explore our website to learn more!
